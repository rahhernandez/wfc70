Welcome to WFC 70

The purpose of WFC 70 is to introduce and strength your data literacy skills. In this course, you will working with data sets, R and R Studio and understanding what data literacy is. This website aims to be a place where we demonstrate solutions to common mistakes, provide more practice, and provide resources to improve your R and data literacy skills.
